Eric Tsai 蔡侑霖

|關於 About|

出生於1997年，現於AI新創公司擔任AI工程師。因過去參與攝影社團活動接觸創作，以往主要以底片攝影、藝術書作為創作手段。近期結合自身專業，開始嘗試數位藝術創作，主要聚焦於大數據、人工智慧與人類互動之下產生的火花。

Born in 1997, currently working as an AI engineer at an AI startup. Previously involved in photography club activities, mainly focused on film photography and art books as creative mediums. Recently, combining my expertise with digital art, with a particular focus on the sparks generated between big data, artificial intelligence and human interaction.
